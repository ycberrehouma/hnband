Bio

"I am music lover, so you can see spinning Techno to tech-house and deep house or even find some metal tunes on top of it.
Music is my oxygen, it's part of my identity"

When it comes to Yassine, It's 100% about music. Not a specific genra or style.
It's a whole journey that will make you dance smartly but let forget about the moment and fly high.
Music is a world where feelings are its flavors. Just be ready for some uniqueness.

Based in NYC since 2019, New to the electronic scene and djing comparing to his peers, but he's been a huge music passionate since the age of 12.
It all came from the metal music and Metallica to be precise. That where his background was born. From there, he became music addicted, never leave home without his headphones. It's what makes his life interesting. Then he went to explore another genra while in high school which is the rap and trap music. Isn't until he graduated from college that he really got to understand the electronic music and since then he never looked back and became a frequent dancer at clubs.

Here he is a DJ himself who can play for hours and never feel like enough, "time stops there so why should i". Currently, i'm in the one year challenge that i set between myself and I, started on the 29th of June 2019 of doing a new set every single week, different genra, theme and vibe where each track is being played only once.

Welcome to Yassine's journey where he's getting closer a step a day to where he doesn't know about its direction, destiny or main goal. But the thing that he's sure about, is "i wanna be anywhere where music is taking good care of me."
